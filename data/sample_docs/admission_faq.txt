COLLEGE ADMISSION FAQ - ACADEMIC YEAR 2024-25

=== B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology) ===

Q: What are the eligibility criteria for B.Tech admission?
A: Candidates must have passed 10+2 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects with a minimum aggregate of 60% marks. SC/ST/OBC candidates get a 5% relaxation. Valid JEE Main score is required.

Q: What is the last date to apply for B.Tech?
A: The last date to apply for B.Tech is July 15, 2024. Applications received after this date will not be considered. Apply online at admissions.college.edu.

Q: What is the application fee for B.Tech?
A: The application fee is Rs. 1,000 for General/OBC candidates and Rs. 500 for SC/ST/PWD candidates. Fee is non-refundable.

Q: What documents are required for B.Tech admission?
A: Required documents: 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates, JEE Main scorecard, transfer certificate, migration certificate, character certificate, 6 passport size photographs, Aadhar card, caste certificate (if applicable).

Q: What is the B.Tech fee structure?
A: Tuition fee: Rs. 1,20,000 per year. Development fee: Rs. 15,000 per year. Exam fee: Rs. 5,000 per year. Total annual fee: approximately Rs. 1,40,000. Fee is subject to revision.

=== MBA (Master of Business Administration) ===

Q: What are the eligibility criteria for MBA?
A: Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline with minimum 50% aggregate marks from a recognized university. Final year students can also apply. Valid CAT/MAT/GMAT score required.

Q: What is the last date to apply for MBA?
A: The application deadline for MBA is April 30, 2024. Late applications may be considered on a case-by-case basis with a late fee of Rs. 500.

Q: What is the MBA fee structure?
A: MBA total program fee is Rs. 3,50,000 for two years. This includes tuition, library, and lab fees. Hostel charges are separate.

Q: What is the MBA admission process?
A: The admission process involves: online application, written test (CAT/MAT score), group discussion, and personal interview. Final merit list is based on 40% academics + 30% entrance score + 30% GD/PI.

=== M.Tech (Master of Technology) ===

Q: What are the eligibility criteria for M.Tech?
A: Candidates must hold a B.Tech/B.E. degree in the relevant discipline with minimum 60% marks. Valid GATE score is required. Students with GATE score above 500 are eligible for scholarship.

Q: What is the last date to apply for M.Tech?
A: Application deadline for M.Tech is June 20, 2024. 

Q: What is the M.Tech fee?
A: M.Tech annual fee is Rs. 80,000 for GATE qualified students. Students with GATE scholarship receive a stipend of Rs. 12,400 per month from AICTE.

=== B.Sc / M.Sc ===

Q: What are the eligibility criteria for B.Sc?
A: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology with 55% aggregate. Merit-based admission.

Q: What is the last date to apply for B.Sc?
A: B.Sc application deadline is June 30, 2024.

Q: What are the eligibility criteria for M.Sc?
A: B.Sc in relevant subject with minimum 55% marks. Admission through entrance exam held in May every year.

=== Hostel and Accommodation ===

Q: How much is the hostel fee?
A: Boys hostel fee: Rs. 60,000 per year (includes accommodation and mess charges). Girls hostel fee: Rs. 65,000 per year. AC room charges are Rs. 20,000 extra per year. Mess charges: Rs. 35,000 per year included.

Q: Is hostel accommodation guaranteed?
A: Hostel accommodation is available for first-year students on priority basis. Allotment is based on distance from hometown and merit. Out-of-state students are given priority.

Q: What facilities are provided in the hostel?
A: Facilities include: furnished rooms, 24/7 Wi-Fi, laundry service, gym, indoor games room, reading room, backup power supply, CCTV surveillance, and 24-hour security.

=== Scholarships ===

Q: What scholarships are available?
A: Available scholarships: Merit scholarship (top 10% students get 25% fee waiver), Sports scholarship (state/national level players), SC/ST government scholarship, Minority scholarship, AICTE scholarship for GATE qualified students.

Q: How to apply for scholarship?
A: Scholarship applications must be submitted within 30 days of admission. Fill the scholarship form available at the accounts office or download from the college website. Attach all required documents including income certificate.

=== Entrance Exams and Cutoffs ===

Q: What is the JEE Main cutoff for B.Tech?
A: General category cutoff: 75 percentile. OBC-NCL: 65 percentile. SC: 45 percentile. ST: 40 percentile. EWS: 65 percentile. Cutoffs may vary by branch.

Q: Which branches are available in B.Tech?
A: Available B.Tech branches: Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), Civil Engineering (CE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Information Technology (IT), Chemical Engineering (CHE).

Q: What is the intake capacity for B.Tech CSE?
A: B.Tech CSE intake: 120 seats (60 General, 30 OBC, 18 SC, 9 ST, 3 EWS). Lateral entry: 12 seats.
