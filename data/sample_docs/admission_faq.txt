COLLEGE ADMISSION FAQ - SAMPLE KNOWLEDGE BASE
==============================================
Source: Admissions Office | Category: General

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA
---------------------
B.Tech (Bachelor of Technology):
- Minimum 60% marks in 10+2 (PCM - Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
- Valid JEE Main score required for top colleges
- Age limit: 17-25 years as on October 1st of admission year
- SC/ST candidates get 5% relaxation in minimum marks

MBA (Master of Business Administration):
- Bachelor's degree in any discipline with minimum 50% marks
- Valid CAT/MAT/XAT score required
- Work experience of 0-2 years preferred (not mandatory for freshers)
- Age limit: No upper age limit

MCA (Master of Computer Applications):
- B.Sc (CS/IT/Maths) or BCA or B.Tech with minimum 55% marks
- Mathematics as one subject in graduation is mandatory
